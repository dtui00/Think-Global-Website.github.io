ABOUT US

Think Global is a New Zealand not-for-profit orgnisation that offers international internship programs. Our purpose is to enable university students and graduates to “broaden their horizons’” through our select variety of internship programs across the globe.

We offer programs in almost all academic disciplines which takes place in Hong Kong, New York, Barcelona, Britain, Beijing, Sydney, South Korea and Barcelona.

Our utmost priority is to bridge the path from university to our participants’ career paths along with being in touch with a different culture.

    OUR VISION & MISSION

As an organisation, our core values are respect, acceptance, and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists in the world. We envision a world where everyone is free to have their own convictions and accepts that others have the right to live in peace, regardless of the diversity of their beliefs, identity or way of life.

  Think Global recognises and cannot emphasise enough that intolerance stems from ignorance and fear of different cultures, nations, or religions. Thus, Think Global provides exchange programs that contribute to the understanding and learning of societies with a different culture, religion, political, or economic system.

WHAT YOU'LL GAIN

Participants in our programs will most definitely develop their professional and personal skills whilst pursuing their passions and dreams. Listed below are key benefits which comes from doing an intern abroad with us:

  • The chance to explore, experience and to embrace how different countries are like. For example, their government systems, traditions, etiquette, religion and culture.

  • The ability to gain a competitive edge and stand out from others when pursuing whichever career path.

  • Through the gained experiences, you'll be able to get out of your comfort zone and grow as a person.

  • A chance to network with others and make connections with people from overseas.

  • First-hand experience into your interested career path and the opportunity to see if it's the right path.

  • Interning abroad promotes self-sufficiency and independence as it will prepare you for adulthood or living away from family & friends.
